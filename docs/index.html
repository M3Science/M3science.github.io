About The Post

The Particle Post is built on a simple conviction: the best science deserves the best communication. We sit at the intersection of rigorous physics, technical craft, and editorial precision — serving researchers, curious readers, and organisations who refuse to compromise on depth. To tell a compelling story, you need to understand the science. To understand the science, you need to read the papers. We do that reading for you — and then we distill it into clear, engaging narratives that capture the wonder and significance of the research.

Founded by a physicist and science communicator with a background in mathematics and physics, The Particle Post approaches every project the way a physicist approaches a problem — first principles, no shortcuts, clarity above all.

The name is intentional. A particle is the smallest meaningful unit of matter. A post is the smallest meaningful unit of communication. We care about getting both right — at every scale, every time.

Think of us as your concierge for the scientific frontier: the team that does the deep reading so your audience gets the signal without the noise.
